Fukuoka / 0 leave days

Can Fukuoka after Friday work still feel restful?

I went straight from the office to the airport and used carry-on luggage only. A hotel near Hakata Station kept the first night easy.

The key decision was the Sunday return time. Arriving home around 22:20 left enough sleep before work.

Tips

  • Use carry-on only for Friday departures.
  • Choose a hotel near airport-line stations.
  • Avoid dawn returns before important Monday meetings.
